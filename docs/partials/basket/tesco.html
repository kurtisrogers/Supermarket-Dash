Tesco does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card — your items are copied to clipboard.
  2. Click Open store to go to tesco.com/groceries.
  3. Sign in and link your Clubcard to see Clubcard Prices.
  4. Use the search bar for each item — paste the name from your copied list.
  5. Add each product to your basket. Clubcard Prices apply automatically at checkout.

Tip: Tesco's "Buy again" section can speed up repeat shops once you've ordered once.
