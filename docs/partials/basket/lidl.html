Lidl grocery is primarily in-store. Here is the easiest workflow:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Print the list or keep it on your phone.
  3. Visit your nearest Lidl — use the store finder.
  4. Download Lidl Plus for in-app coupons and cashback on selected items.
  5. Scan the Lidl Plus app at checkout to activate offers.

Tip: Lidl's middle aisle (Lidlina) has rotating non-food bargains worth a look.
