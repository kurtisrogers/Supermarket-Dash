Ocado does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Click Open store to go to ocado.com.
  3. Sign in to your Ocado account.
  4. Search each item and add to basket — Ocado stocks M&S products exclusively online.
  5. Smart Pass subscribers get free delivery on orders over the minimum spend.

Tip: Ocado's "Regulars" feature remembers your usual items for faster repeat orders.
