Waitrose does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Click Open store to go to waitrose.com.
  3. Sign in with your myWaitrose account for member offers.
  4. Search each item and add to basket.
  5. myWaitrose offers include free hot drinks in-store when you make a purchase.

Tip: Waitrose often price-matches on own-brand vs premium — compare unit prices.
