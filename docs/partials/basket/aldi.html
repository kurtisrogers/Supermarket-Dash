Aldi grocery is primarily in-store. Online ordering is limited to Specialbuys. Here is the easiest workflow:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Print the list or keep it on your phone.
  3. Visit your nearest Aldi store — use the store finder.
  4. Aldi has no loyalty card — the low shelf price is the price you pay.
  5. Bring your own bags — Aldi charges for carrier bags.

Tip: Aldi's Super 6 fruit & veg deals change weekly — check the aisle ends for extra savings.
