Sainsbury's does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Click Open store to go to sainsburys.co.uk.
  3. Sign in and link your Nectar card to unlock Nectar Prices.
  4. Search each item by name and add to basket.
  5. Nectar Prices are applied when your card is linked — check the orange Nectar Price label on products.

Tip: Download the Nectar app to collect points in-store too.
