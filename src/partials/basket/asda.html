Asda does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Click Open store to go to asda.com/groceries.
  3. Sign in to your Asda account (optional but saves your basket).
  4. Search each item and add to basket.
  5. Asda Rewards cashback is credited after purchase — not shown as a shelf price.

Tip: Check the Asda Rewards app for personalised cashback offers before you shop.
