Morrisons does not expose a public add-to-basket API. Here is the fastest way to add your list:

  1. Click Copy list on the savings map card.
  2. Click Open store to go to groceries.morrisons.com.
  3. Sign in and add your More Card to see member prices.
  4. Search each item and add to basket.
  5. More Card offers appear when signed in — look for the More Card price badge.

Tip: Morrisons often has good fresh produce deals in-store even when online prices differ.
